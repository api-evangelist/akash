Akash Mainnet 14 upgrades the network to Cosmos SDK v0.53, introducing JWT authentication, IAVL storage optimizations, expedited governance, multi-depositor escrow, and lease termination reasons. The jump from v0.45 addresses nearly eight years of accumulated technical debt.
